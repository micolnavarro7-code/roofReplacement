Our Services

Complete Roof Replacement for Middlesex County Homes

Our team provides full roof replacement solutions — from complete tear-offs and architectural shingle upgrades to metal roofing and flat roof systems — protecting your home for decades to come.

Your roof is your home's most critical defense against the elements. When shingles are past their lifespan, patching stops working and a full replacement becomes the smartest investment. New England's harsh winters, ice storms, and summer heat make choosing the right materials and installer essential.

Full Tear-Off & Replacement

We strip the old system down to the deck, inspect for rot, and install a complete new roof with ice-and-water shield, synthetic underlayment, and proper ventilation.

Architectural Shingles

Upgrade from basic three-tab to dimensional architectural shingles that offer superior wind resistance, longer warranties, and a premium look that boosts curb appeal.

Metal Roofing

Standing seam and metal panel systems that last 50+ years with virtually zero maintenance. Ideal for New England snow shedding and energy efficiency.

Flat Roof & EPDM Systems

Commercial-grade rubber membrane and TPO systems for low-slope sections, dormers, and additions — fully sealed and warrantied against ponding water.

Every project starts with a free inspection. We assess your roof's condition, identify the best material for your home and budget, and provide a clear, itemized proposal — no high-pressure sales, just honest advice from a licensed team.
